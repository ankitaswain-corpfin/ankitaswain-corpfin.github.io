Featured

If my Medium pieces had a group chat, it would be equal parts macroeconomics, memes, and “wait, but what does this mean for my money?”

I write about finance and crypto the way I wish someone had explained them to me. Curious, skeptical, and allergic to jargon. My articles tend to zoom out from the daily price noise and look at the mechanisms underneath. It explains how protocols are designed, where incentives are hiding, and what all of this means for risk, regulation, and real people.

I’m particularly interested in the messy middle between traditional finance and web3. The tokenization, payment systems, financial inclusion, and the very human behaviors that drive markets. Blogging is my way of thinking in public, testing ideas, and translating complex concepts into stories that feel practical, honest, and just a little bit fun.
